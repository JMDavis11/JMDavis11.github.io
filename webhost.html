Jace's Web Hosting Review

The following are all components of the review.

Bluehost

Bluehost is a web hosting company owned by Endurance International Group. It is one of the 20 largest web hosts, collectively hosting well over 2 million domains with its sister companies, HostMonster, FastDomain and iPage (via Wikipedia).

  • Price - Starting at $2.95 per month. This is a special introductory offer and there is a 30-day money-back guarantee.
  • Hosting Services - Shared Hosting, VPS Hosting, Dedicated Hosting
  • Domain Services - Each domain includes easy management through a simple interface, auto renewal to protect your domain from expiring, and domain lock to prevent unauthorized transfers.
  • Options - Free domain name for first year, free SLL certificate included, Wordpress install, and 24/7 professional support.

GoDaddy

GoDaddy is an American publicly traded Internet domain registrar and web hosting company, headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona, and incorporated in Delaware. As of March 2019, GoDaddy has approximately 18.5 million customers and over 9,000 employees worldwide (via Wikipedia).

  • Hosting Services - Web Hosting ($5.99/mo), Wordpress Hosting ($6.99/mo), VPS Hosting ($24.99/mo).
  • Domain Services - .social ($19.99), .net ($14.99), .com ($11.99).
  • Options - Website templates, Website builder, email plans, hosting plans, SLL certificate.

HostGator

HostGator is a Houston-based provider of shared, reseller, virtual private server, and dedicated web hosting with an additional presence in Austin, Texas (via Wikipedia).

  • Hosting Services - 1 Year domain registration on shared annual plans

    • - The Hatchling Plan ($2.75/mo) comes with a single domain, WordPress accessibility, unmetered bandwidth, and a SSL certficate.

    - The Baby Plan ($3.95/mo) comes with unlimited domains, Wordpress accessibility, unmetered bandwidth, and a SSL certificate.

    - The Business Plan ($5.95/mo) comes with unlimited domains, upgrade ability to positive SSL, a dedicated IP, and SEO tools.

  • Domain Services - .com ($12.95/yr); .website ($9.95/yr); .org ($12.95/yr)
  • Options - website builder, WordPress hosting, VPS hosting, Dedicated hosting

Personal Pick

I think Bluehost would be the best option for a person that is new to the world of website creation. It has low starting prices, and a 30-day money-back guarantee. It offers various hosting and domain options and has the credentials as a well-respected, successful company.
